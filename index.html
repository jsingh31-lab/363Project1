The Winter Experience in Syracuse

Syracuse is transformed into a winter wonderland of snow-covered beauty with fun winter activities. Ice skating at community rinks, sledding down snowy hills on a sled, or plodding through trails for a day of skiing action is just a taste of what the whole family can become involved in. Dress warmly, get chilly, and have fun!
